We are **Team SaaSsy**, and we secured **3rd place** out of **47 teams** at the [GDG Baku](https://gdg.community.dev/gdg-baku/) [Micro SaaS Hackathon](https://gdg.community.dev/events/details/google-gdg-baku-presents-micro-saas-hackathon/), which brought together **over 100 participants**! Our demo product, **Rashot**, helped us stand out.

Big thanks to [Fidan Azimova](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fidan-azimova/) and [Vagif Aliyev](https://www.linkedin.com/in/vagif-aliyev-a5733a275/) for the teamwork, and to everyone who made this event possible.
